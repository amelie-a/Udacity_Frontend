“There are certain very stabilizing forces in gardening
that can ground us when we are feeling shaky.”
– Andrew Keshner, MarketWatch

This is a blog on plants and urban gardening in times of COVID.

When the pandemic hit, most of us were called to stay at home. What was once normal, was suddenly forbidden. Life was shifted to the inside. As a student living in a shared flat, this had dramatic consequences. I found myself being scrammed into my tiny room all day every day. "How can I make my life more bearable?" is what I asked myself one day, looking up from my laptop into my room. This was when I decided to bring plants into my life. After finally moving into a bigger room, I have now created a collection of green friends. Here I am introducing each of them and giving hints on how to treat indoor plants right.
