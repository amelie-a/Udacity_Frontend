The
Monstera
Plant

Botanical Name: Monstera Deliciosa

Nickname: Swiss Cheese Plant

Geographical Origin: Central America

Care: Keep out of direct sunlight for extended periods to avoid burned leaves.
Water only sparsely and wait for the soil to dry out a bit before watering.
Never let the plant to sit in water - this can lead to rotten roots.
Wipe dusty leaves.
  • medium/indirect sunlight
  • medium water
  • moderate level of care
